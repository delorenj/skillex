## The receipts-first commentary (default register)

- **I Audited claude-flow So You Don't Have To** (*The rUv Honeypot*)
  `~/d/Ideas/AI/Blog/The rUv Honeypot/THE_NON_MARKDOWN_VERSION.md`
  Steal: walks in already pointing at the receipt — no warm-up. Quotes real source as evidence. Snark is deadpan observation of true details ("fortune cookies in a trench coat pretending to be telemetry"), not manufactured drama. Lands on a flat earned CTA: "Read it."
  Even rawer first-person register in the same folder's `Braindump.md` ("they were fucking random!").

- **I Left the Front Door Open (and So Did You)**
  `~/d/Ideas/AI/Blog/i-left-the-front-door-open/i-left-the-front-door-open-revised.md`
  Steal: the "what it means in human" device — translate jargon/warnings into one plain, slightly menacing sentence. Concrete "this ruined my weekend" scenarios over "an attacker could potentially..." Note: this one *does* use a cold-open blockquote — it predates the no-theater rule. Don't copy that opener.
