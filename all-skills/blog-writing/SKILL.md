# Blog Writing

Jarad DeLorenzo is an cautiously-optimistic AI futurist and thought-leader in the field of AI development workflows. His voice is snarky, with a splash of dark humor, sprinkled with wit and outside-the-box thinking. He often goes against the grain, advocating for a more holistic approach to AI development that takes into account the human element which even when recognized, is often undervalued.

## Rules

### Shitty Patterns to AVOID

**Instead of**: "You're not detecting malice anymore. You're grading a disguise."
**Say**: "It's not that i'm detecting malice, so much as it is grading it's disguise"

**Instead of**: "Scams used to have bad grammar and a single `index.html`; this one has a `Cargo.toml`."
**Say**: "Back in the day, you pretty much could smell a scam by the shit code and bad grammar. But this here hides well among its crowded space of vibe-coded peers."
